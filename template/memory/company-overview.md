# Company Overview

Acme Commerce is a D2C e-commerce company founded in 2022, currently Series B
with ~85 employees. They sell premium widgets through three channels: direct
(Shopify, 65% of revenue), Amazon FBA (25%), and wholesale (10%).

Key operational characteristics:
- Manufacturing via Shenzhen Components (single-source risk on circuit boards)
- Fulfillment through ShipStation + LA warehouse (owned) and Amazon FBA
- Tech stack centers on Shopify + NetSuite + HubSpot with a fragile Zapier
  integration between Shopify and HubSpot that breaks monthly
- Three teams: Operations (Sarah Chen), Engineering (David Park), Sales (Rachel Green)
